Traditional Foods

Here are some Jakarta's traditional foods.

By David Bintang October, 2020

KERAK TELOR

Kerak Telor is a native Jakarta (Betawi) food, with ingredients of white glutinous rice, chicken or duck eggs, dry roasted ebi (dried marinated shrimp) plus fried shallots, then topped with mashed spices

By David Bintang October, 2020

PECEL

Pecel or pecal is a food combined with peanut sauce seasoning as the main ingredient and mixed with various types of vegetables.

By David Bintang October, 2020

SOTO BETAWI

Soto Betawi is a popular soup in the Jakarta area. Like Soto Madura and soto sulung, Soto Betawi also uses offal.
