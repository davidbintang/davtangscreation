Tourist Destinations

Here are some Jakarta's tourist destinations.

By David Bintang October, 2020

ANCOL

Taman Impian Jaya Ancol is the largest and most comprehensive thematic recreational park in Jakarta. Located in North Jakarta facing the Java Sea, Taman Impian Jaya Ancol is a beachfront resort area that sits on an area of ​​80 hectares.

By David Bintang October, 2020

MONAS

The National Monument or popularly abbreviated as Monas or Tugu Monas is a 132 meter (433 ft) high memorial monument erected to commemorate the resistance and struggle of the Indonesian people to seize independence from the colonial rule of the Dutch East Indies.

By David Bintang October, 2020

RAGUNAN

Ragunan Zoo is a zoo located in the Ragunan area, Pasar Minggu, South Jakarta, Indonesia. This 140 hectare zoo was founded in 1864. Inside, there are various collections consisting of 295 species and 4040 specimens.
